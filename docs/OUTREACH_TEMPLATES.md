## Messaging Strategy

### Opening Line Rules
‚úÖ **Good:**
- "I saw your post about reducing electricity costs..."
- "Your home in [neighborhood] looks perfect for solar..."
- "Most people don't know they could save 5M VND/month..."

‚ùå **Bad:**
- "You might be interested in solar..." (too generic)
- "I'm offering a 50% discount..." (they'll think it's spam)
- "Hello, are you looking for solar?" (no personalization)

### Key Selling Points
**Always mention:**
1. **Cost saving** (numbers: 3-5M VND/month)
2. **Quick ROI** (~3-4 years payback)
3. **Time-bound action** (limited slots, quote expires, etc.)
4. **Social proof** (50+ customers, 25-year warranty, etc.)

### Call to Action
**Always end with:**
- Clear next step (fill quote / call me / site visit)
- Time-bound (this week / next 3 days)
- Low barrier entry ("5 minutes", "free", "no obligation")
