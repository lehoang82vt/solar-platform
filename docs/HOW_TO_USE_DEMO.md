## Common Usage Questions

### Q: I don't know my roof azimuth. What do I do?

**A:** Easy options:
1. Use Google Maps satellite view + compass
2. Watch where the sun hits your roof
   - Morning light = facing East (90¬∞)
   - Afternoon light = facing West (270¬∞)
   - All day light = facing South (180¬∞)
3. Just estimate - we'll measure precisely on site visit

### Q: How accurate is the demo quote?

**A:** Very accurate for design, ¬±5-10% on price because:
- Uses Google satellite data for sun exposure
- Quotes current equipment prices (updated weekly)
- Final price confirmed on site visit (usually same ¬±5%)

### Q: Can I edit the quote after?

**A:** Yes! We can:
- Add/remove panels (change from 20 to 30 panels, etc.)
- Add/remove batteries
- Change inverter size
- Adjust tilt angle after site visit

### Q: Do I need to be at home for the site visit?

**A:** Yes. We need to:
- Check roof condition & safety
- Measure exact dimensions
- Check electrical panel location
- Get approval for placement
- Takes about 1-2 hours

### Q: What if my quote is too expensive?

**A:** Common solutions:
1. **Smaller system** - 5 kWp instead of 10 kWp
   - Costs less upfront (80M instead of 155M)
   - Still saves 2+ million VND/year
2. **Add batteries later** - Buy grid-tied system now, add storage in 5 years
3. **Payment plans** - Discuss with our team (some partners offer 3-5 year financing)
